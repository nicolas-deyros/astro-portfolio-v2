# Technology Stack: Nicol√°s Deyros Portfolio

## Core Framework & Language

- **Framework:** [Astro 5.x](https://astro.build/) - Utilizing Islands Architecture for optimal performance and hybrid rendering (SSR + SSG).
- **UI Library:** [React 19](https://react.dev/) - Powering complex interactive components ("islands") such as the contact form and AI features.
- **Language:** [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) - Ensuring type safety across the entire codebase.

## Styling & UI

- **CSS Framework:** [Tailwind CSS 4.x](https://tailwindcss.com/) - Utility-first styling with the latest performance-focused engine.
- **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) - Providing smooth, accessible transitions and interactions.

## Data & Content

- **Database:** [Astro DB](https://docs.astro.build/en/guides/astro-db/) - Managed SQLite database for structured data like admin sessions or dynamic content.
- **Content Management:** [Astro Content Collections](https://docs.astro.build/en/guides/content-collections/) - Type-safe Markdown/MDX handling for blog posts and site data.

## Artificial Intelligence

- **On-Device AI:** [Chrome AI APIs](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/ai/built-in-ai) - Leveraging built-in browser capabilities for summarization and translation to maintain privacy and performance.

## Testing & Quality Assurance

- **Unit & Integration Testing:** [Vitest](https://vitest.dev/) - Fast, Vite-native testing framework.
- **End-to-end & Performance Testing:** [Puppeteer](https://pptr.dev/) & [Playwright](https://playwright.dev/) - For cross-browser testing and performance auditing (Lighthouse).

## Infrastructure & Deployment

- **Platform:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) - Edge-first deployment with native support for Astro's SSR and database integration.
